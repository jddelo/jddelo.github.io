Map Samples | Hippie Vector Tiles



This map shows the proposed Union Cycliste International Bike Route on a shaded relief basemap. The bike route spans over 2,000 miles from Bar Harbor, ME to Clearwater, FL and is broken down into daily stages of about 100 miles/day. The basemap was prepared from combining DEM and bathymetric data sources and manipulating those to highlight relief. Landcover data was also used for the basemap coloring.
