Map Samples | Studying the Effect of Drought on Wildfires in the United States



This map illustrates the effect of drought on the occurance and severity of wildfires from 2000-2018. It was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3, and the Esri-Leaflet Library as a group project for the final project in Geography 575 - Interactive Cartography & Visualization.
